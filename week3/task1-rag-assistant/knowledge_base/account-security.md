# NovaCart — Account & Security

## Creating an Account
You can sign up with your mobile number or email. A one-time password (OTP) verifies your identity. A NovaCart account lets you track orders, save addresses, and manage returns.

## Resetting Your Password
1. On the login page, select Forgot Password.
2. Enter your registered email or mobile number.
3. Enter the OTP sent to you and set a new password.
Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include a letter and a number.

## Updating Profile and Addresses
- Manage your name, email, phone, and saved addresses under My Account.
- You can save multiple delivery addresses and set a default.

## Account Security
- NovaCart uses OTP-based login and never asks for your password over phone or email.
- Enable login alerts to be notified of new sign-ins.
- If you notice suspicious activity, change your password and contact support immediately.

## Privacy and Data
- Your personal data is used only to process orders and improve your experience.
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support; deletion is completed within 30 days.
- NovaCart does not sell your personal data to third parties.

## Beware of Scams
NovaCart will never call or message asking for your OTP, full card number, CVV, or UPI PIN. Do not share these with anyone claiming to be from NovaCart.
