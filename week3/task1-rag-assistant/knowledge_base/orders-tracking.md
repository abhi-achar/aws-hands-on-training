# NovaCart — Orders & Tracking

## Order Statuses
An order moves through these statuses:
1. Placed — order received and payment confirmed.
2. Confirmed — inventory reserved and being prepared.
3. Dispatched — handed to the courier; tracking link is active.
4. Out for Delivery — arriving today.
5. Delivered — successfully delivered.
6. Cancelled — order cancelled by you or by NovaCart.

## Tracking Your Order
- Open My Orders and select the order to see live status.
- A tracking link is also sent by email and SMS when the order is dispatched.
- Tracking updates can take a few hours to reflect after dispatch.

## Modifying an Order
- You can change the shipping address or items only while the order is in the Placed status.
- Once an order is Confirmed or Dispatched, it cannot be modified. You can return it after delivery instead.

## Cancelling an Order
- Orders can be cancelled for free any time before they are Dispatched.
- After dispatch, cancellation is not possible, but you can refuse delivery or return the item.
- Refunds for cancelled prepaid orders are initiated immediately and follow the standard refund timeline.

## Missing or Partial Orders
If your order arrives with missing items, or an item is part of a multi-shipment order, check the order details page — multi-item orders may ship separately and arrive on different days. If an item is still missing after all shipments are delivered, contact support within 48 hours.
