# NovaCart — Payments & Billing

## Accepted Payment Methods
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, American Express)
- Net banking from major Indian banks
- NovaCart Wallet
- Cash on Delivery (available on orders up to INR 20,000)

## Why a Payment Might Fail
Payments can fail for several reasons:
- Incorrect card details or expired card
- Insufficient balance or credit limit reached
- Bank declined the transaction or flagged it as suspicious
- One-time password (OTP) not entered in time
- Temporary network or gateway timeout

If your payment fails but money was deducted, it is an automatic hold that the bank reverses within 5–7 business days. No order is created for a failed payment, so you can safely retry.

## EMI and Pay Later
- No-cost EMI is available on eligible products for orders above INR 5,000 with select bank cards.
- Pay Later options are offered through partner providers at checkout, subject to eligibility.

## Invoices
A tax invoice is emailed after the order is delivered. You can also download it anytime from My Orders.

## Refunds to Original Method
Refunds always go back to the original payment method used at checkout. For Cash on Delivery, refunds go to your NovaCart Wallet or a verified bank account.

## Security
NovaCart never stores your full card number or CVV. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways with 3D Secure authentication.
