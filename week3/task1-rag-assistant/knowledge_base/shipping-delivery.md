# NovaCart — Shipping & Delivery

## Shipping Options and Timelines
NovaCart offers three shipping methods within India:

| Method | Delivery Time | Cost |
|--------|---------------|------|
| Standard | 4–6 business days | Free on orders over INR 499, otherwise INR 49 |
| Express | 2–3 business days | INR 99 |
| Priority | 1 business day (metro cities only) | INR 199 |

Orders are processed Monday to Saturday. Orders placed after 6 PM IST are processed the next business day.

## Order Cutoff and Dispatch
- Orders confirmed before 6 PM IST are dispatched the same day.
- You receive a tracking link by email and SMS once the order is dispatched.
- Priority delivery is available only in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Pune.

## International Shipping
NovaCart currently ships only within India. International shipping is not available.

## Delivery Delays
Delivery may be delayed during sale events, public holidays, or due to weather and courier disruptions. If your order has not arrived 2 business days after the estimated date, contact support with your order ID.

## Failed Delivery
If a delivery attempt fails, the courier retries up to 2 more times on consecutive days. After 3 failed attempts the package returns to our warehouse and a refund is issued to the original payment method.
